Onsite Support Services

Welcome to InfoCare!

InfoCare is a market leader in providing innovative information technology services and support solutions. Services are our first and only business. Our focus is providing superior support services for our partners and customers.

We handle more than 400.000 service activities yearly. InfoCare offers:

  • The best geographical coverage in Denmark, Finland, Norway and Sweden through our service locations and an extensive service partner network
  • InfoCare deliver on-site support within 4 hours, and 1 hour for selected clients in all Nordic countries
  • Highly skilled and certified technicians
  • A full suite of service offerings across a broad product and technology range
  • An advanced service management suite featuring real-time updates from our field service technicians,  advanced planning and dispatch solutions to manage service events, web access and direct interfaces with major freight carriers
  • 24×7 availability
